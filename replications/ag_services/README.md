### Abstract

Agricultural service delivery - through extension officers, cooperative organizations, input provision, mechanization support, irrigation advice, credit facilitation, and post-harvest assistance - shapes the choices farmers make and the technologies they adopt. This study uses community-level service indicators harmonized across GLSS rounds, statistical matching, and a meta-stochastic frontier framework to evaluate how variation in local agricultural support environments relates to farm-level technology adoption and technical efficiency. *Full abstract to follow once v001 lands.*
