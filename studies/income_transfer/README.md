### Abstract

Income transfers — whether from public programmes, remittances, or NGO
interventions — relax liquidity constraints and can plausibly shift
farmer investment, technology adoption, and managerial effort. This
study will use the harmonized GLSS income-transfer modules together
with statistical matching and a meta-stochastic frontier framework to
estimate how income transfers translate into changes in crop production
technology and technical efficiency.
